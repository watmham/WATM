Worked All Tai'an Modes (WATM) Award

泰安全模式通联奖项

项目介绍 | Project Introduction

WATM奖项是为泰安业余无线电爱好者设立的专属通联奖项，旨在记录和鼓励爱好者在泰安地区完成多种通信模式的通联操作。

The WATM Award is an exclusive communication award for amateur radio enthusiasts in Tai'an, aiming to record and encourage enthusiasts to complete communication operations in various modes in Tai'an area.

网站访问 | Website Access

本奖项的官方展示页面：https://watm-award.github.io/WATM/

Official display page of this award: https://watm-award.github.io/WATM/

奖项规则 | Award Rules

  • 参与者需为合法的泰安业余无线电爱好者；
  • 需在泰安地区完成至少5种不同通信模式的通联记录；
  • 通联记录需真实有效，可提供相关日志作为凭证。
  • Participants must be legitimate amateur radio enthusiasts in Tai'an;
  • Need to complete communication records of at least 5 different modes in Tai'an area;
  • Communication records must be true and valid, and relevant logs can be provided as proof.
