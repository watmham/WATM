通联泰安全模式奖状申请规则

Worked All Tai'an Modes (WATM) Award Application Rules

版本 1.0 | 2026 年 2 月 16 日

Version 1.0 | February 16, 2026

第1条：目的

为鼓励各地业余无线电爱好者与中国山东省泰安之业余电台通联，支持业余泰安无线电发展，让更多ham参与业余无线电活动，由此设立“通联泰安全模式奖状”奖状。

To encourage amateur radio enthusiasts worldwide to communicate with amateur stations in Tai'an, Shandong Province, China, support the development of amateur radio in Tai'an, and engage more HAMs in amateur radio activities, the "Worked All Tai'an Modes (WATM) Award" is hereby established.

第2条：申请要求

  • 申请者必须为合法设立的业余电台。
  • 通联（收听）对象须为中国泰安（包括泰山区、岱岳区、宁阳县、东平县、新泰市、肥城市）的业余电台，或临时来泰安设置的业余电台。
  • 用于申请奖状的有效通联应当发生于北京时间 2026 年 2 月 20 日之后。
  • 通联必须符合法律法规之要求，不能通过地面中继台或互联网完成通联。
  • Applicants must be legally licensed amateur radio stations.
  • The contacted (or heard) stations must be amateur stations located in Tai'an, China (including Taishan District, Daiyue District, Ningyang County, Dongping County, Xintai City, and Feicheng City), or temporary amateur stations set up in Tai'an.
  • Valid contacts for award application must occur after February 20, 2026 (Beijing Time).
  • All contacts must comply with relevant laws and regulations, and shall not be made via terrestrial repeaters or the Internet.

第3条：通联组合

每个通联组合由对方电台呼号、波段、模式确定。业余卫星通信、月面反射通信、流星余迹通信等特殊类型通信额外计算通联组合。

Each contact combination is defined by the remote station's call sign, band, and mode. Special communication modes, such as amateur satellite communications, EME (Earth-Moon-Earth), and meteor scatter, are counted as additional combinations.

普通组合

使用包括CW、RTTY、SSB、FM、SSTV、弱信号模式（如FT8、JT65等）、CP16；

Contacts using modes including CW, RTTY, SSB, FM, SSTV, weak-signal modes (e.g., FT8, JT65), and CP16.

特殊组合

使用卫星、月面反射通信、使用1.2Ghz及以上波段、3.5MHz及以下波段、远距离（直线距离大于1000km且不使用hf，3.5MHz波段除外）、小于5w；

Contacts via satellite, EME, on bands of 1.2 GHz or above, on bands of 3.5 MHz or below, long-distance (straight-line distance > 1000 km, excluding HF except 3.5 MHz), or with power output < 5W.

第4条：通联证明

以下类型的通联证明可用于申请该奖状：

  • 纸质 QSL 卡片，申请时需提供正反面之扫描图像。
  • ARRL Logbook of the World（LoTW）、eQSL确认证明，申请时需提供 QSO Detail 之截图。
  • Paper QSL cards: Scanned images of both front and back sides must be provided.
  • Confirmations from ARRL Logbook of the World (LoTW) or eQSL: Screenshots of QSO Details must be provided.

第5条：奖状类型及要求

一般奖状

  • 初级奖：与至少 1 个记录的泰安业余电台完成任意 3 个普通组合的通信模式且双向 QSO，并取得通联证明。
  • 银奖：与至少 5 个记录的泰安业余电台完成任意 5 个普通组合，或在特殊组合、和普通组合中各任意选择 2 个通信模式且双向 QSO，并取得通联证明。
  • 金奖：与至少 15 个记录的泰安业余电台完成各 5 个普通、特殊组合的双向 QSO，并取得通联证明。
  • Junior Award: Complete 2-way QSOs with at least 1 recorded Tai'an amateur station using any 3 general combinations, and obtain valid proof.
  • Silver Award: Complete 2-way QSOs with at least 5 recorded Tai'an amateur stations using any 5 general combinations, or any 2 general and 2 special combinations, and obtain valid proof.
  • Gold Award: Complete 2-way QSOs with at least 15 recorded Tai'an amateur stations using 5 general and 5 special combinations each, and obtain valid proof.

特别奖状

  • QSL 卡收集特别奖：和记录的泰安业余电台完成双向 QSO，并获得 5 种不同设计的纸质 QSL 卡。
  • Eyeball QSL 特别奖：拜访泰安业余电台，并获得 3 个业余电台的 Eyeball QSL 卡。
  • SWL 特别奖：收听到至少 3 次泰安业余电台的通联，并获得相应的 QSL 卡。
  • 青少年 SWL 特别奖：收听到至少 2 次泰安业余电台的通联，并获得相应的 QSL 卡。该奖项申请人需不满 18 周岁。
  • QSL Card Collection Special Award: Complete 2-way QSOs with recorded Tai'an amateur stations and obtain 5 distinct paper QSL card designs.
  • Eyeball QSL Special Award: Visit Tai'an amateur stations and obtain Eyeball QSL cards from 3 different stations.
  • SWL Special Award: Hear at least 3 Tai'an amateur station contacts and obtain corresponding QSL cards.
  • Youth SWL Special Award: Hear at least 2 Tai'an amateur station contacts and obtain corresponding QSL cards. Applicants must be under 18 years of age.

第6条：申请方式

访问 watm-award.github.io/WATM/ 下载申请表，将填写好的申请表和按顺序整理打包好的通联证明副本发送至 watmham@outlook.com。

Visit watm-award.github.io/WATM/ to download the application form. Fill out the form and package it with ordered copies of contact proof, then send to watmham@outlook.com.

第7条：真实性验证

  • 申请人需承诺提供的通联信息真实有效、可供查证。必要时，奖状管理员可要求申请者提供通联证明的原件。若申请者无法按要求提供通联证明，申请作废。
  • 必要时，奖状管理员可要求申请人提供对方电台为学校设置的业余电台的佐证材料。若无法证明，相关 QSO 不会计入奖项。
  • 申请者提交虚假通联信息或伪造通联证明的，取消其申请和已经签发的奖状，并禁止其再次申请奖状。
  • Applicants must ensure all contact information is true, valid, and verifiable. The Award Manager may request original proof when necessary. Failure to provide proof will result in application invalidation.
  • If requested, applicants must provide evidence that a contacted station is a school-based amateur station; otherwise, such QSOs will not be counted.
  • Submission of false contact information or forged proof will result in immediate cancellation of the application and any awarded certificates, and permanent ban from future applications.

第8条：奖状

  • 奖状大小为 A4，载有奖状名称和信息、申请者姓名、呼号、奖状编号、签发日期、奖状管理员署名等。
  • 奖状有电子和纸质两种形式。申请电子奖状的，不收取费用。申请纸质奖状的，中国大陆境内收取人民币15元工本费和邮寄费，以挂号信寄出；中国大陆境外收取人民币40元工本费和邮寄费，以航空挂号信寄出。
  • 奖状由奖状管理员小组成员轮流签发。
  • Certificates are A4-sized, featuring the award name, applicant's name, call sign, certificate number, issue date, and signatures of Award Managers.
  • Two formats are available: Electronic Certificate (free of charge); Paper Certificate (RMB 15 for mainland China, RMB 40 for overseas, including printing and postage, sent via registered or registered airmail).
  • Certificates are issued on a rotating basis by members of the Award Manager Team.

第9条：规则

此规则由奖状管理员小组负责解释，如有问题请联系 watmham@outlook.com。

These rules are interpreted by the Award Manager Team. For inquiries, contact watmham@outlook.com.
