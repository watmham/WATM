通联泰安全模式奖申请规则 WATM Award Application Rules

一、设立目的

为鼓励各地业余无线电爱好者与中国山东省泰安之业余电台通联，支持泰安业余无线电发展，让更多ham参与业余无线电活动，特此设立通联泰安全模式奖（WATM）。

To encourage amateur radio enthusiasts worldwide to communicate with amateur stations in Tai'an, Shandong Province, China, support the development of amateur radio in Tai'an, and engage more HAMs in amateur radio activities, the Worked All Tai'an Modes Award (WATM) is hereby established.

二、申请要求

  • 申请者必须为合法设立的业余电台；
  • 通联（收听）对象须为中国泰安（含泰山区、岱岳区、宁阳县、东平县、新泰市、肥城市）的业余电台，或临时来泰安设置的业余电台；
  • 用于申请奖状的有效通联应发生于北京时间2026年2月20日之后；
  • 通联符合法律法规要求，允许使用本地合法中继台，不得通过互联网完成通联。
  • Applicants must be legally licensed amateur radio stations;
  • Contacted (or heard) stations must be amateur stations in Tai'an, China (including Taishan District, Daiyue District, Ningyang County, Dongping County, Xintai City, Feicheng City), or temporary amateur stations set up in Tai'an;
  • Valid contacts for award application must occur after February 20, 2026 (Beijing Time);
  • All contacts comply with relevant laws and regulations, use of local legal repeaters is permitted, and contacts via the Internet are prohibited.

三、通联组合

每个通联组合由对方电台呼号、波段、模式确定；业余卫星通信、月面反射通信、流星余迹通信等特殊类型通信额外计算通联组合。

Each contact combination is defined by the remote station's call sign, band and mode; special communications such as amateur satellite, EME, meteor scatter are counted as additional combinations.

普通组合：CW、RTTY、SSB、FM、SSTV、弱信号模式（FT8、JT65等）、CP16；

General Combinations: CW, RTTY, SSB, FM, SSTV, weak-signal modes (FT8, JT65, etc.), CP16;

特殊组合：卫星通信、月面反射通信、1.2GHz及以上波段、3.5MHz及以下波段、远距离（直线距离＞1000km且不使用HF，3.5MHz波段除外）、发射功率＜5W；

Special Combinations: Satellite communication, EME, bands above 1.2GHz, bands below 3.5MHz, long-distance (straight-line distance ＞1000km, excluding HF except 3.5MHz), transmit power ＜5W;

四、奖状类型及要求

一般奖

  • 初级奖：与至少1个记录的泰安业余电台完成任意3个普通组合的双向QSO，并取得通联证明；
  • 银奖：与至少5个记录的泰安业余电台完成任意5个普通组合，或普通/特殊组合各任意2个的双向QSO，并取得通联证明；
  • 金奖：与至少15个记录的泰安业余电台完成各5个普通、特殊组合的双向QSO，并取得通联证明。
  • Junior Award: Complete 2-way QSOs with at least 1 recorded Tai'an amateur station using any 3 general combinations and obtain valid proof;
  • Silver Award: Complete 2-way QSOs with at least 5 recorded Tai'an amateur stations using any 5 general combinations, or 2 general & 2 special combinations, and obtain valid proof;
  • Gold Award: Complete 2-way QSOs with at least 15 recorded Tai'an amateur stations using 5 general & 5 special combinations each, and obtain valid proof.

特别奖

  • QSL卡收集特别奖：与记录的泰安业余电台完成双向QSO，并获得5种不同设计的纸质QSL卡；
  • Eyeball QSL特别奖：拜访泰安业余电台，并获得3个业余电台的Eyeball QSL卡；
  • SWL特别奖：收听到至少3次泰安业余电台的通联，并获得相应的QSL卡；
  • 青少年SWL特别奖：不满18周岁者收听到至少2次泰安业余电台的通联，并获得相应的QSL卡。
  • QSL Card Collection Special Award: Complete 2-way QSOs with recorded Tai'an amateur stations and obtain 5 distinct paper QSL card designs;
  • Eyeball QSL Special Award: Visit Tai'an amateur stations and obtain Eyeball QSL cards from 3 stations;
  • SWL Special Award: Hear at least 3 Tai'an amateur station contacts and obtain corresponding QSL cards;
  • Youth SWL Special Award: Applicants under 18 years old hear at least 2 Tai'an amateur station contacts and obtain corresponding QSL cards.

五、通联证明

  • 纸质QSL卡片：需提供正反面扫描图像；
  • ARRL LoTW、eQSL确认证明：需提供QSO Detail截图。
  • Paper QSL cards: Scanned images of both front and back sides are required;
  • ARRL LoTW & eQSL confirmations: Screenshots of QSO Detail are required.

1. Download the application form via the link above and fill it out completely;
2. Send the filled form + sorted contact proof copies to the designated email: watmham@outlook.com;
3. Electronic certificate is free, paper certificate charges for production and postage.

七、真实性验证

  • 申请人承诺通联信息真实有效、可供查证，必要时需提供通联证明原件，无法提供则申请作废；
  • 必要时需提供对方学校业余电台的佐证材料，无法证明则相关QSO不计入奖项；
  • 提交虚假信息/伪造证明者，取消申请及已签发奖状，永久禁止再次申请。
  • Applicants confirm all contact information is true and verifiable; original proof may be required if necessary, failure to provide will invalidate the application;
  • Evidence of school-based amateur stations may be required if necessary, unproven QSOs will not be counted;
  • Applicants who submit false information or forged proof will have their application and issued certificates cancelled, and be permanently banned from future applications.
