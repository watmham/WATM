通联泰安全模式奖申请规则 WATM Award Application Rules

一、设立目的

为鼓励各地业余无线电爱好者与中国山东省泰安之业余电台通联，支持泰安业余无线电发展，让更多ham参与业余无线电活动，特此设立通联泰安全模式奖（WATM）。

To encourage amateur radio operators worldwide to contact amateur stations in Tai'an, Shandong Province, China, support the development of amateur radio in Tai'an, and engage more HAMs in amateur radio activities, the Worked All Tai'an Modes (WATM) Award is hereby established.

二、申请要求

  • 申请者必须为合法设立的业余电台；
  • 通联（收听）对象须为中国泰安（含泰山区、岱岳区、宁阳县、东平县、新泰市、肥城市）的业余电台，或临时来泰安设置的业余电台；
  • 用于申请奖状的有效通联应发生于北京时间2026年2月20日之后；
  • 通联符合法律法规要求，允许使用本地合法中继台，不得通过互联网完成通联。
  • Applicants must hold a valid amateur radio license;
  • Stations contacted (or monitored) must be amateur stations located in Tai'an, China (including Taishan District, Daiyue District, Ningyang County, Dongping County, Xintai City, Feicheng City), or temporary stations operated in Tai'an;
  • Valid contacts for award application must be made after February 20, 2026 (Beijing Time);
  • All contacts must comply with applicable laws and regulations. Use of local legal repeaters is permitted. Contacts via the Internet are not allowed.

三、通联组合

每个通联组合由对方电台呼号、波段、模式确定；业余卫星通信、月面反射通信等特殊类型通信额外计算通联组合。

Each contact combination is defined by the call sign, band, and mode of the remote station. Special communications such as amateur satellite and EME (Earth-Moon-Earth) are counted as additional contact combinations.

普通组合：CW、RTTY、SSB、FM、SSTV、弱信号模式（FT8、JT65等）、CP16；

General Combinations: CW, RTTY, SSB, FM, SSTV, weak-signal modes (FT8, JT65, etc.), CP16;

特殊组合：卫星通信、月面反射通信、1.2GHz及以上波段、3.5MHz及以下波段、远距离（直线距离＞1000km且不使用HF，3.5MHz波段除外）、发射功率＜5W；

Special Combinations: Satellite communication, EME, bands 1.2 GHz and above, bands 3.5 MHz and below, long-distance (straight-line distance > 1000 km without using HF, except 3.5 MHz band), transmit power < 5 W;

四、奖状类型及要求

一般奖

  • 初级奖：与至少1个记录的泰安业余电台完成任意3个普通组合的双向QSO，并取得通联证明；
  • 银奖：与至少5个记录的泰安业余电台完成任意5个普通组合，或普通/特殊组合各任意2个的双向QSO，并取得通联证明；
  • 金奖：与至少15个记录的泰安业余电台完成各5个普通、特殊组合的双向QSO，并取得通联证明。
  • Junior Award: Complete 2-way QSOs with at least 1 registered Tai'an amateur station using any 3 general combinations and provide valid proof;
  • Silver Award: Complete 2-way QSOs with at least 5 registered Tai'an amateur stations using any 5 general combinations, or 2 general and 2 special combinations, and provide valid proof;
  • Gold Award: Complete 2-way QSOs with at least 15 registered Tai'an amateur stations using 5 general and 5 special combinations respectively, and provide valid proof.

特别奖

  • QSL卡收集特别奖：与记录的泰安业余电台完成双向QSO，并获得5种不同设计的纸质QSL卡；
  • Eyeball QSL特别奖：拜访泰安业余电台，并获得3个业余电台的Eyeball QSL卡；
  • SWL特别奖：收听到至少3次泰安业余电台的通联，并获得相应的QSL卡；
  • 青少年SWL特别奖：不满18周岁者收听到至少2次泰安业余电台的通联，并获得相应的QSL卡。
  • POTA特别奖：在泰安地区获得编号的POTA公园进行10次有效QSO。
  • 远征特别奖：非梅登黑德网格定位法OM86的地区前往OM86网格进行通联。对象非泰安电台的需提交20个QSO记录，泰安电台需提交10个QSO记录。
  • DX特别奖：位于泰安地区的电台获得直线距离超2000km的1个QSO即可申请。
  • 高山DX特别奖：在泰山风景名胜区境内开展通联活动，获得直线距离超400km的3个QSO即可申请。所有操作均应符合景区管理规定。
  • QSL Card Collection Special Award: Complete 2-way QSOs with registered Tai'an amateur stations and receive 5 differently designed paper QSL cards;
  • Eyeball QSL Special Award: Visit Tai'an amateur stations and receive Eyeball QSL cards from 3 stations;
  • SWL Special Award: Monitor at least 3 contacts of Tai'an amateur stations and receive corresponding QSL cards;
  • Youth SWL Special Award: Applicants under 18 years old monitor at least 2 contacts of Tai'an amateur stations and receive corresponding QSL cards;
  • POTA Special Award: Make 10 valid QSOs at a numbered POTA park in the Tai'an area;
  • Expedition Special Award: Operate from the OM86 Maidenhead grid for stations located outside OM86. Submit 20 QSO records for non-Tai'an stations, 10 QSO records for Tai'an stations;
  • DX Special Award: Stations located in Tai'an can apply with 1 QSO exceeding 2000 km in straight-line distance;
  • Alpine DX Special Award: Operate within Mount Tai Scenic Area and make 3 QSOs exceeding 400 km in straight-line distance. All operations must comply with scenic area regulations.

五、通联证明

  • 纸质QSL卡片：需提供正反面扫描图像；
  • ARRL LoTW、eQSL确认证明：需提供QSO Detail截图。
  • Paper QSL cards: Scanned images of both front and back sides are required;
  • ARRL LoTW & eQSL confirmations: Screenshots of QSO Detail are required.

1. 点击下方链接下载申请表，按要求填写完整；

1. Download the application form via the link above and fill it out completely;
2. Send the completed form plus sorted contact proof copies to the designated email: watmham@outlook.com;
3. Certificates are available in electronic or paper form. Electronic certificates are free. Paper certificates cost ¥15 for production plus ¥4.2 for postage.

七、真实性验证

  • 申请人承诺通联信息真实有效、可供查证，必要时需提供通联证明原件，无法提供则申请作废；
  • 提交虚假信息/伪造证明者，取消申请及已签发奖状，永久禁止再次申请。
  • Applicants certify that all contact information is true, valid, and verifiable. Original proof may be required if necessary; failure to provide it will invalidate the application;
  • Applicants who submit false information or forged documents will have their applications and issued certificates revoked, and will be permanently banned from future applications.
